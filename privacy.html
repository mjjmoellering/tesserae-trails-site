Tesserae Trails

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 June 2026

Tesserae Trails is a single-player puzzle game that runs entirely on your device. This policy explains, in plain language, what data the app does and does not handle.

The short version

Tesserae Trails does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no trackers, and no third-party SDKs. The app does not connect to the internet to play.

Data stored on your device

Your puzzle progress (completed levels, best star counts, and daily-puzzle records) is saved locally on your device so the game can remember where you left off. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or anyone else. Deleting the app removes it.

No data collection

The app contains no networking code and makes no network requests. It does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, photos, or any other personal information.

Children's privacy

The app is rated suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If the app changes in a way that affects this policy, this page will be updated and the date above revised.
