Common questions

How do I play?

Jump one tile over a neighbouring tile and onto a third. The middle tile is absorbed and the jumping tile blends its colour into where it lands. Combine the primaries into secondaries and reduce the board until a single tile remains. There's a full step-by-step walkthrough on the How to Play page.

I skipped the tutorial — can I see it again?

The in-app tutorial only appears on first launch and can't be replayed yet, but every lesson is preserved on the How to Play page, complete with animated examples of each move.

What are stars for?

You earn stars by solving puzzles efficiently. Accumulated stars unlock later trails in a campaign. Each daily puzzle keeps its own record and adds to your total.

Does the game need an internet connection?

No. Tesserae Trails plays entirely offline. There are no accounts, ads, or tracking.

Will my progress sync between devices?

Progress is stored locally on each device. There is currently no cross-device sync.
