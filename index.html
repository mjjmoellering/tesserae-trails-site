Tesserae Trails

A calm glass-tile colour puzzle for iPhone

Each tile is a pane of coloured glass. Jump one tile over a neighbour and onto a third: the middle tile is absorbed and your tile blends its colour into where it lands. Mix the three primaries into secondaries and, finally, into a single glowing piece.

  • Handcrafted puzzles and a themed campaign of trails
  • A fresh daily puzzle every day
  • Luminous glass visuals, fluid animation, gentle haptics
  • No accounts, no ads, no tracking
  • Plays entirely offline
